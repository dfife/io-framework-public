Paper 32: Horizon Support Clamp Local Acceleration Audit

Question
- At the physical hidden-support clamp R = r_s with Rdot = 0, what is Rddot on
  the local support equation implied by the Paper 1 / 3 torsion-Lambda law?

Executive result
- derived: on the local support variable, the Paper 1 / 3 torsion-Lambda term
  scales as 1/R^2 and contributes only a constant to Rdot^2.
- derived: therefore it cancels out of the local acceleration equation.
- derived:
    Rddot = - c^2 r_s / (2 R^2)
  and at the clamp
    Rddot(r_s) = -6.722177851434710e-11 m s^-2.

So the local support tendency at the clamp is inward, not outward.
