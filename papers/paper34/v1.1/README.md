# Paper 34 v1.1 Reproducibility Bundle

Classification: `verified / public-reproducibility-support`

This bundle contains the curated scripts, frozen outputs, constants, and audit
reports needed to reproduce the Paper 34 v1.1 Hubble-tension scorecard and
anti-fit checks.

It is intentionally not a mirror of the private research lab. Exploratory route
searches, killed-route scans, private scratch state, and manuscript drafts are
excluded.

## Contents

- `scripts/01_compute_hext_grid.py`
  - Computes the full `(alpha,n)` grid for the Paper 34 `H_ext` formula.
- `scripts/02_compare_to_published_measurements.py`
  - Computes six-method H0 pulls against imported published comparison values.
- `scripts/03_run_anti_fit_check.py`
  - Reproduces the anti-fit backstop table.
- `scripts/04_validate_expected_outputs.py`
  - Fast validation of the frozen result files included in this bundle.
- `data/imported_constants.json`
  - Framework constants and published H0 comparison values.
- `results/`
  - Frozen JSON outputs generated by the scripts.
- `reports/`
  - Kappa-style structural audit report and JSON.

## Claim Boundary

- `verified`: arithmetic grid generation, scorecard pull calculation, frozen
  output validation, and anti-fit table reproduction.
- `derived/scoped`: Paper 34's extension-premise package as stated in the
  manuscript and kappa-audit report.
- `not included`: private route-search scripts, failed route scans, draft
  manuscripts, and non-public scratch state.
- `not claimed`: the scripts do not prove the extension premises by themselves.
